1. Introduction

Welcome to Cravier ("we," "our," or "us"). Cravier is a social restaurant discovery and posting mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information about you when you use the Cravier app ("App").

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

Cravier is operated by Tobey Lee, an individual developer based in Illinois, USA.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Cravier App. It does not apply to third-party websites or services that may be accessible through links within the App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information when you use Cravier:

2.1 Account and Identity Information

  • Email address (collected via Firebase Authentication when you register)
  • Display name or username (chosen by you at account setup)
  • Profile photo (uploaded by you, optional)

2.2 User-Generated Content

  • Posts and captions you create and publish within the App
  • Restaurant reviews and ratings you submit
  • Photos you upload or capture using your device camera

2.3 Restaurant Interaction Data

  • Restaurants you save, view, or interact with within the App
  • Taps, searches, and browsing activity related to restaurant discovery features

2.4 Location Data

  • Precise GPS location data, collected only when you grant the App location permission
  • Location data is used solely to display and search for restaurants near you
  • You may deny or revoke location permission at any time through your iOS device settings
  • Denying location permission will disable the nearby restaurant search feature but will not prevent use of other App features

2.5 Device and Camera Access

  • Camera access: requested only when you choose to take a photo for a post; no photos are accessed without your initiation
  • Photo library access: requested only when you choose to upload a photo from your device library

2.6 Analytics and Diagnostic Data

  • App usage events (e.g., features used, screens visited, buttons tapped)
  • Session data (e.g., session length, frequency of use)
  • Crash reports and diagnostics collected via Firebase Crashlytics
  • Device type, operating system version, and other technical identifiers associated with analytics data

2.7 Payment Information

If you subscribe to a paid tier within the App, payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. We do not directly collect, store, or process your payment card numbers or financial account details.

2.8 Information Not Collected

We do not collect the following:

  • Social Security numbers or government-issued identification numbers
  • Precise biometric data
  • Health or medical information
  • Data about children under the age of 13 (see Section 10)

Firebase provides authentication, database (Firestore), file storage, analytics, and crash reporting services. Data processed through Firebase is subject to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

9. Your Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA and CalOPPA)

This section describes privacy rights available under California law, including the California Consumer Privacy Act as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CCPA/CPRA) and the California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA). These rights are available to California residents. However, we extend these rights as a matter of policy to all users of the App regardless of residency.

9.1 California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA) Rights

California residents have the following rights with respect to their personal information:

Right to Know. You have the right to request that we disclose: (a) the categories of personal information we have collected about you; (b) the categories of sources from which we collected your personal information; (c) our business or commercial purpose for collecting your personal information; (d) the categories of third parties with whom we share your personal information; and (e) the specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.

Right to Delete. You have the right to request that we delete personal information we have collected about you, subject to certain exceptions. We will comply with verified deletion requests unless retention is necessary to: complete a transaction, detect security incidents, debug errors, exercise free speech or other legal rights, comply with a legal obligation, or for other purposes permitted by the CCPA/CPRA.

Right to Correct. You have the right to request that we correct inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.

Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising purposes. Accordingly, there is no opt-out mechanism required for this practice at this time. If our practices change, we will update this policy and provide the required opt-out mechanism.

Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information. The CCPA/CPRA provides rights to limit the use of certain sensitive personal information. We do not use or disclose sensitive personal information for purposes beyond those permitted by the CCPA/CPRA.

Right to Non-Discrimination. We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA/CPRA rights. We will not deny you goods or services, charge different prices, provide a different level of service, or suggest that you will receive a different level of service because you exercised your privacy rights.

12. Location Data

The App may request access to your device's precise location (GPS) to enable the nearby restaurant search feature. Location access is entirely optional and controlled by you through your iOS device settings.

We collect location data only when: (a) you have granted the App permission to access your location; and (b) you are actively using a location-dependent feature within the App. We do not track your location in the background.

Location data is transmitted to our servers and to the Google Maps / Google Places API solely to fulfill the restaurant search request you initiate. We do not use your location data to build profiles for advertising purposes.

You can revoke location permission at any time by navigating to: Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Cravier on your iOS device.
